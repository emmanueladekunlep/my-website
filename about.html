About Me

Full Stack Developer, Author & Branding Strategist

Who I Am

Emmanuel Adekunle Peace – also known as Sam Transcend – is a Full Stack Developer, Author of 25+ books, and Branding Strategist from Nigeria.

He is the founder and CEO of LabelReach Advertising Ltd, a firm dedicated to helping schools, small businesses, and creative professionals grow through smart branding, strategic marketing, and innovative full-stack web development solutions.

With nearly two decades of experience across the education and business sectors, Peace believes that every individual has the power to scale their impact when given the right tools and knowledge.

His mission is to prevent costly mistakes, unlock hidden potential, and build a culture of peace and prosperity – one innovative strategy at a time.
