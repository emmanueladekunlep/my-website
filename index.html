About Me

Emmanuel Adekunle Peace – also known as Sam Transcend – is a passionate digital education advocate, entrepreneur, and branding strategist from Nigeria.

He is the founder and CEO of LabelReach Advertising Ltd, a firm dedicated to helping schools, small businesses, and creative professionals grow through smart branding, strategic marketing, and innovative digital solutions.

With nearly two decades of experience across the education and business sectors, Peace believes that every individual has the power to scale their impact when given the right tools and knowledge.

🌟 His mission is to prevent costly mistakes, unlock hidden potential, and build a culture of peace and prosperity – one innovative strategy at a time.

What I Do

  • 📚 Teach digital skills – Hands‑on coding and web development lessons for young learners.
  • 📈 Provide growth & marketing consultancy – Helping schools and brands attract customers, build trust, and increase revenue.
  • 🎤 Conduct workshops and seminars – Speaking on digital education, personal branding, and creative strategy.
  • ✍️ Write and publish educational content – Articles, workbooks, and social media posts that make complex topics simple.
